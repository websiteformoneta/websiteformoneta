The Problem

Margin Loss Is Built Into Cloud Reseller Billing

AWS and Azure resellers operate across multiple customers, pricing models, and layered discount programs. Billing, pricing, and cost are rarely aligned, resulting in margin loss across customer accounts.

Without infrastructure built for cloud resale operations, billing becomes manual, discounts are inconsistently applied, and margins cannot be reliably measured or controlled.

Discount programs are difficult to track and inconsistently applied

Customer pricing is disconnected from actual cloud costs

Billing relies on spreadsheets and manual reconciliation

Misapplied discounts and pricing gaps reduce margins

Limited visibility into margin performance by customer

Margin loss is not isolated. It compounds across every customer account.
